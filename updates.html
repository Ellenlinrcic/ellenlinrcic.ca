Biometrics Are Not Always “Once and Done”

Biometrics are generally valid for ten years for temporary residence applications, but most permanent residence applicants must provide biometrics each time they apply.

生物信息是否需要重新采集，取决于申请类型。临时居民申请通常十年有效， 但多数永久居民申请通常需要每次重新提供 biometrics。
