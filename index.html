About Ellen

Jie “Ellen” Lin is a Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant based in Richmond, British Columbia. She provides immigration consulting services to clients in English and Chinese.

Ellen works with individuals, families, students, workers, and employers to assess immigration options, prepare application strategies, organize supporting documents, and respond to immigration concerns such as refusals or procedural fairness letters.

Her approach is practical, detail-oriented, and focused on helping clients understand both the opportunities and risks involved in their applications.

Why Work With Ellen
为什么选择 Ellen

Immigration applications are not only about filling out forms. A strong application requires strategy, consistency, supporting evidence, and a clear understanding of the applicant’s circumstances.

移民申请不仅仅是填写表格。一个完整而有说服力的申请，通常需要清晰的策略、 前后一致的文件、充分的支持证据，以及对申请人实际情况的准确理解。
